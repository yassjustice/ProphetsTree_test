The Sacred Prophetic Lineage: From Adam to Muhammad
Peace Be Upon Them All
Based on Quran & Sahih Hadith
Adam (آدم)
أبو البشر
First Prophet • Father of Humanity
Wife: Hawwa (Eve/حواء)
Created by Allah from clay, given knowledge of all names, first to receive divine revelation. Father of all humanity. Resided in Paradise before being sent to Earth as the first human and first prophet.
Children
Habil (Abel/هابيل) - The righteous son, first martyr
Qabil (Cain/قابيل) - First to commit murder
Shith (Seth/شيث) - Prophet, successor to Adam
Many other children according to Islamic tradition
Shith (شيث)
Prophet • Successor to Adam
Third son of Adam, appointed as prophet after Abel's death. Continued Adam's message and guidance to early humanity. Received divine scrolls and established righteous communities.
Idris (إدريس)
Prophet • Raised to High Station
Early prophet mentioned in Quran, raised to high station by Allah. Known for his knowledge, righteousness, and scholarship. Some identify him with Enoch from biblical tradition.
Nuh (نوح)
Prophet & Messenger • Builder of the Ark
Wife: Righteous believer (previous wife among disbelievers)
Sent to guide his people for 950 years. Built the ark by Allah's command during the great flood. Saved believers and pairs of animals, establishing a new chapter for humanity.
Sham - Ancestor of Semitic peoples
Ham - Ancestor of African peoples
Yafith - Ancestor of European peoples
Ibrahim (إبراهيم)
خليل الله
Prophet & Messenger • Friend of Allah
Wives:
Sarah (سارة) - Mother of Ishaq
Hajar (هاجر) - Mother of Isma'il
Called "Khalilullah" (Friend of Allah). Broke the idols of his people, survived being thrown into fire by Nimrod. Built the sacred Ka'bah with Isma'il. Father of the prophetic nations and establisher of monotheism.
Sons - Both Prophets
Isma'il (إسماعيل) - Ancestor of Arabs, helped build Ka'bah
Ishaq (إسحاق) - Ancestor of Israelites, father of Ya'qub
Isma'il (إسماعيل)
Prophet • Ancestor of Arabs
Son of Ibrahim and Hajar. Helped build the sacred Ka'bah in Makkah. Ancestor of Arab tribes including Quraysh, the tribe of Prophet Muhammad. Known for patience and complete obedience to Allah.
Ancestor of Arab tribes
Including Quraysh (Muhammad's tribe)
Builder of Ka'bah with his father
Ishaq (إسحاق)
Prophet • Father of Ya'qub
Son of Ibrahim and Sarah. Miracle birth in their old age as promised by Allah. Father of Ya'qub (Israel). Ancestor of the Israelite prophets and the twelve tribes of Israel.
Ya'qub (Israel) - Father of 12 tribes
Ancestor of Israelite prophets
Ya'qub (يعقوب)
إسرائيل
Prophet • Israel • Father of 12 Tribes
Wives: Raheel, Lea, and others
Son of Ishaq, grandson of Ibrahim. Father of twelve sons who became the twelve tribes of Israel. Given the title "Israel" by Allah. Known for his patience during separation from Yusuf.
The Twelve Sons
Yusuf (يوسف) - Prophet, ruler of Egypt
Binyamin (بنيامين) - Beloved youngest brother
Yahuda (يهوذا) - Tribe of Judah
Ruubeel (روبيل) - Eldest son
And eight other sons forming the twelve tribes
Yusuf (يوسف)
Prophet • The Truthful • Ruler of Egypt
Wife: Zulaikha (later became righteous)
Son of Ya'qub, sold into slavery by jealous brothers. Rose to become ruler of Egypt through divine dream interpretation. Reunited with family and forgave his brothers. Known for extraordinary beauty, wisdom, and patience through trials.
Musa (موسى)
Prophet & Messenger • Speaker with Allah
Wife: Daughter of Shu'ayb
Brother: Harun (Aaron) - Prophet and helper
Saved from Pharaoh as a baby, raised in the palace. Received the Torah and Ten Commandments on Mount Sinai. Led the Israelites out of Egypt through the miraculous parting of the Red Sea. Known for direct communication with Allah.
Harun (هارون)
Prophet • High Priest • Helper of Musa
Brother of Musa, served as his assistant and spokesman to Pharaoh. First high priest of the Israelites. Known for his eloquence and unwavering support of his brother's divine mission.
Dawud (داوود)
Prophet & King • Psalmist
Wives: Multiple wives including mother of Sulayman
King and prophet of Israel. Given the Zabur (Psalms) with beautiful divine hymns. Defeated the giant Goliath (Jalut) with a sling. Known for his melodious voice in worship and just rule over his kingdom.
Sulayman (سليمان) - Prophet and King
Sulayman (سليمان)
Prophet & King • Master of Jinn and Animals
Notable Wife: Queen of Sheba (Bilqis)
Son of Dawud. Given dominion over jinn, animals, and wind by Allah. Built the magnificent Temple in Jerusalem. Known for unparalleled wisdom, immense wealth, and the most glorious kingdom in history.
Zakariya (زكريا)
Prophet • Guardian of Maryam
Wife: Ishba (Elizabeth)
Guardian of the blessed Maryam (Mary). Given the miracle son Yahya in his old age as a sign from Allah. Known for his devotion and care of the holy sanctuary.
Yahya (يحيى) - Prophet, John the Baptist
Yahya (يحيى)
Prophet • John the Baptist • The Forerunner
Son of Zakariya, cousin of 'Isa (Jesus). First to recognize and confirm 'Isa's prophethood. Known for exceptional piety, chastity, and asceticism from his youth. Preached repentance and preparation for 'Isa's mission.
'Isa (عيسا)
Prophet & Messenger • The Messiah
Mother: Maryam (Mary/مريم) - Most virtuous woman
Born miraculously without a father through Allah's command. Given the Gospel (Injil) and performed many miracles including healing the sick and raising the dead. Ascended to heaven alive and will return before the Day of Judgment.
Muhammad (محمد)
رسول الله ﷺ
Final Prophet & Messenger • Seal of Prophets • The Chosen One
Lineage: Quraysh tribe, descended from Isma'il ibn Ibrahim
Father: 'Abdullah ibn 'Abdul-Muttalib (died before birth)
Mother: Aminah bint Wahb (died when Muhammad was 6)
Guardians: 'Abdul-Muttalib (grandfather), then Abu Talib (uncle)
The Mothers of Believers (أمهات المؤمنين)
Khadijah bint Khuwaylid (خديجة) - First wife, first believer, mother of most children. Supported him through early revelations.
'A'ishah bint Abi Bakr (عائشة) - Daughter of Abu Bakr, youngest wife, scholar and narrator of hadith.
Hafsah bint 'Umar (حفصة) - Daughter of 'Umar ibn al-Khattab, keeper of Quran manuscripts.
Zaynab bint Khuzaymah (زينب) - Known as "Mother of the Poor" for her charity.
Umm Salamah Hind (أم سلمة) - Wise counselor, known for her intelligence.
Zaynab bint Jahsh (زينب) - Cousin of the Prophet, marriage established social reforms.
Juwayriyah bint al-Harith (جويرية) - Her marriage brought peace with Banu Mustaliq tribe.
Umm Habibah Ramlah (أم حبيبة) - Daughter of Abu Sufyan, symbol of reconciliation.
Safiyyah bint Huyayy (صفية) - From noble Jewish family, promoted unity.
Maymunah bint al-Harith (ميمونة) - Last wife married, known for piety.
Sawdah bint Zam'ah (سودة) - Second wife, helped raise the Prophet's children.
The Blessed Children
Sons: Al-Qasim (القاسم), 'Abdullah (عبد الله) [also called at-Tayyib, at-Tahir], Ibrahim (إبراهيم) - all died in infancy
Daughters (all from Khadijah):
Zaynab (زينب) - Eldest daughter, married to Abu al-'As
Ruqayyah (رقية) - Married to 'Uthman ibn 'Affan
Umm Kulthum (أم كلثوم) - Also married to 'Uthman after Ruqayyah
Fatimah az-Zahra (فاطمة الزهراء) - Most beloved daughter, married to 'Ali
Key Family Members & Descendants
'Ali ibn Abi Talib (علي) - Cousin, son-in-law, fourth Caliph, married to Fatimah
Fatimah az-Zahra (فاطمة) - Beloved daughter, "Leader of women of Paradise"
Al-Hasan (الحسن) - Grandson through Fatimah, second Imam
Al-Husayn (الحسين) - Grandson through Fatimah, third Imam
The Ahl al-Bayt (أهل البيت) - The blessed household of the Prophet
The Final Messenger to All Humanity: Given the Quran as the eternal miracle and final revelation. Established the perfect Islamic community in Madinah. Known as "Al-Amin" (The Trustworthy) even before his prophethood. The Seal of all Prophets - no messenger will come after him until the Day of Judgment. His message completed and perfected the religion for all mankind.

This sacred family tree represents the blessed chain of prophethood as established in the Quran and authentic Sahih Hadith, showing how Allah's divine guidance continued through different nations and epochs, culminating in the final, universal message through Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) - the Seal of Prophets and Mercy to all the Worlds.
